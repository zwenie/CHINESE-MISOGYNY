MISOGYNY IN CHINESE CHARACTERS

Many characters containing the radical "女" (woman) are believed to carry derogatory, negative, and repulsive connotations. Through this exhibition, viewers can examine the issues of misogyny and gender bias in Chinese society using an online visual Chinese character dictionary.
